The Beauty of Burmese Tradition

"Yoe Yar" will always be there for all those who cherish the tradition of Burmese.

Myanmar Handicrafts

Burmese people are known as the people who can create outstanding arts when it comes to handicrafts. There are ten skills in Myanmar traditional handicrafts, which are called "Pan Se Myo (The Ten Flowers)". Among the arts and handicrafts created based on such various crafts, the famous and well-known accessories include the Pathein umbrella, lacquerware, woodcarving, Myanmar puppet, and Myanmat panchi.

Myanmar Traditional Dress

As there are eight ethnic groups in Myanmar, each ethnic group has its own traditional national costume. However, regardless of ethnic group, the general clothing for all Burmese people is the Taipon and Longyi for men and the traditional patterned shirt and Thummy for women.
