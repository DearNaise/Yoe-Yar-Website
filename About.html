About Yoe Yar

Our Story

Our journey began with a passion for preserving and sharing the rich traditions and the cultural heritage of Myanmar.
It all started with the embarkation on a mission to bring the authentic, time-honored products of Myanmar to the world.

Our Vision

We want to be the foremost destination for Burmese traditional and authentic products and promote the true essence of Burmese culture by offering a curated collection of traditional and contemporary products.

Our Mission

We have a desire by working directly with the artisans, we ensure that their skills are passed down to future generations, safeguarding the cultural heritage of Myanmar.

Special Thanks to our customers.

We will continue to try our best to realize our mission. Thank you for joining us on this incredible journey.
